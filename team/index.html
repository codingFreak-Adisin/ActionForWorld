Our Team

The amazing team behind the Action For World

Aditya Sinha

Executive Director & Director of Technology

Aditya Sinha, a 15-year-old student at DAV Public School, Pune, is the Founder of Action For World. He manages the fellowship, leads the fellowship sessions, maintains the website, write articles for Action Blogs, and in general oversees the organisation. Aditya is dedicated to raising awareness about climate change and global warming. His leadership and passion for the environment help drive the mission of Action For World.

Neev Goenka

Head of Operations

A 17-year-old student at GEMS Wellington International School of Dubai, Neev is an enthusiastic environmentalist, who currently leads fellowship sessions for sustainable finance, as well as passionately heads the expansion of the emissions management system, applying his past research work. Starting as a fellowship student, Neev was elected to the executive team due to his creativity and innovative development of an automated marketing system to create climate-aware imagery.
