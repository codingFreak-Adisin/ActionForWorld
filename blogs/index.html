5 Things You Should Know About Forest Management and Carbon Benefits

Forests are home to 80% of life on earth. Forests are the barriers, to keeping us and our planet healthy by utilizing its deepest layers, the top of trees and crowns. They serve as a barrier against erosion, landslides, and avalanches. They give us fresh air, clean the drinking water in our rivers, and attract the rain that would be needed to grow food.

Action For World

Working towards Climate Issues

We are Action For World a youth-led non profit organization working towards spreading awareness about climate change, educating about climate change and environmental science. We work on creating educational resources, course websites and running a community for people who are passionate about climate. We are not just an organization we are a group of teens coming together as one to solve The Climate Crisis.

Outside of climate change courses, we use various mediums to provide education to all. For instance, we run Action Blogs, an active Instagram and 4 - 5 months long fellowship, teaching the fellows about intersectional environmentalism & leadership.
