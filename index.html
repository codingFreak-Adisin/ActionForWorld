Non Profit Organization

We are Action For World a youth-led non profit organization working towards spreading awareness about climate change, educating about climate change and environmental science. We work on creating educational resources, course websites and running a community for people who are passionate about climate. We are not just a organization we are a group of teens coming together as one to solve the climate crisis.

Outside of Climate Change courses, we use various mediums to provide education to all. For instance, we run Action Chat Community, Action Climate Blogs, Action Conferencing App and an active Instagram.

What is Coral Bleaching?

Coral bleaching is the process when corals become white due to various stressors, such as changes in temperature, light, or nutrients.

What is Biodiversity Loss?

Biodiversity loss includes the extinction of species worldwide, as well as the local reduction or loss of species in a certain habitat.

What is Ocean Acidification?

Ocean Acidification is an effect of atmospheric carbon dioxide entering the ocean. Increased acidity can affect plant and marine species in different ways.
