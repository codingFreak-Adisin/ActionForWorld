Non Profit Organization

We are Action For World a youth-led nonprofit organization working towards spreading awareness about climate change and educating about environmental science and climate change.

We work on creating educational resources, courses, and communities for people who are passionate about climate. We are not just an organization we are a group of teens coming together as one to solve the climate crisis.

Outside of courses on Climate Change, we use various mediums to provide education to all. For instance, we run ECIA Fellowship, Action Climate Blogs, and informative posts on Instagram.
